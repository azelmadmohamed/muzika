About Muzica Channel

Muzica morocco is a YouTube channel dedicated to the enchanting sounds of Moroccan world music, playing a variety of unique and attractive musical instruments. Our channel shows diverse country music.

Whether you're a seasoned fan of Moroccan music or new to its unique sounds, our channel has something for everyone. Join us on the journey of listening to all kinds of beautiful Moroccan music, from Rai to Chaabi and other genres The principle of the channel:
Preserving the music while not preserving the video clip and replacing it with a video of our design
